Cereal Recipe

Descrition

Grain food, usually precooked or ready-to-eat, that is customarily eaten with milk or cream for breakfast in the United States and elsewhere, often sweetened with sugar, syrup, or fruit.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Add cereal to bowl
  2. Add milk to bowl
  3. Wait 30 seconds
  4. Enjoy
